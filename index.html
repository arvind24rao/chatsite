What is loop?

loop uses AI architecture for user groups to communicate passively and asynchronously. you choose when to engage without expectations or read receipts, and loop acts like your neighbourhood mom sharing stories.

Narrative updates

Get story-like digests on their lives instead of noisy back-and-forth.

Cleaned tone & context

Your quiet coordinator

A calm “switchboard” that keeps everyone in the loop without pressure.

For families & friends

For big groups

Be in a large class or club without the spam — get the digest when you want it.
